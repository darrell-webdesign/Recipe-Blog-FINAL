Recipe Three: Spicy Garlic Noodles

These spicy garlic noodles are quick, flavorful, and perfect for a fast weeknight dinner.



Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Servings: 2
Recipe Name Spicy Garlic Noodles
Ingredients
  • 8 oz spaghetti or noodles
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 green onions, chopped
Steps
  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Heat butter and olive oil in a pan.
  3. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and chili flakes.
  5. Toss noodles in the sauce.
  6. Top with green onions and serve.
Allergens Gluten, Soy
Nutrition Calories: 420 | Protein: 12g | Carbs: 60g | Fat: 14g
