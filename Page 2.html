Recipe Two: Chicken Quesadillas

Cheesy chicken quesadillas are crispy, flavorful, and great for lunch or dinner.


Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Servings: 3
Recipe Name Chicken Quesadillas
Ingredients
  • 2 cups cooked shredded chicken
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 4 flour tortillas
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon taco seasoning
Steps
  1. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  2. Cook onions until soft.
  3. Add chicken and taco seasoning.
  4. Place mixture on half of a tortilla.
  5. Add cheese and fold tortilla.
  6. Cook in a pan until golden and crispy.
  7. Slice and serve.
Allergens Dairy, Gluten
Nutrition Calories: 450 | Protein: 28g | Carbs: 35g | Fat: 20g
