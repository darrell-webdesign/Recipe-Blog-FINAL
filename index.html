Recipe One: Rice And Beans

Black beans and rice make a great 30-minute vegetarian meal that's filling, delicious, and cheap! Great as a side dish or in a burrito bowl, too.

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Servings: 4
Recipe Name Black Beans and Rice
Ingredients
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • ¾ cup uncooked white rice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 ½ cups low sodium vegetable broth
  • 3 ½ cups canned black beans, drained
Steps
  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and cook until soft.
  3. Stir in garlic and cook for about 1 minute.
  4. Add rice, cumin, cayenne pepper, and salt.
  5. Pour in vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until rice is cooked.
  7. Stir in black beans and cook until heated through.
  8. Serve warm.
Allergens None common (check broth for gluten if sensitive)
Nutrition (per serving) Calories: ~250 | Protein: 10g | Carbs: 45g | Fat: 4g
Tips / Variations
  • Add cilantro or lime juice for extra flavor.
  • Serve in a burrito bowl with avocado.
  • Use brown rice for more fiber.
